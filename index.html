About the Maker

Every Board
Tells a Story

I'm Danny — a woodworker, a perfectionist, and the guy who spends way too long picking the right piece of walnut. TreeHaus started in my garage with a table saw and a love for making things people actually use.

Every board is hand-selected, hand-cut, and finished with food-safe oils. No shortcuts, no particle board, no compromises. Just real wood, real craftsmanship, and something you'll keep for decades.

How It's Made

From Raw Timber to Your Table

01

Select

Hand-picking premium hardwoods — walnut, maple, cherry, and exotic species — for grain, color, and character.

02

Design

Planning the layout, grain direction, and pattern — whether it's end-grain, edge-grain, or a custom shape.

03

Craft

Precision cutting, gluing, clamping, and sanding — each board goes through multiple rounds by hand.

04

Finish

Multiple coats of food-safe mineral oil and beeswax, buffed to a smooth, beautiful sheen.
